# Gartner Public Cloud Services Market Summary 2025

> Source: Gartner Press Release, April 2025 — "Gartner Forecasts Worldwide Public Cloud End-User Spending to Reach $723 Billion in 2025"
> URL: https://www.gartner.com/en/newsroom/press-releases

## Executive Summary

Worldwide end-user spending on public cloud services is forecast to total **$723.4 billion** in 2025, up from **$595.7 billion** in 2024, representing a **21.4% year-over-year growth**. All cloud segments are projected to experience double-digit growth, with IaaS and PaaS leading.

## Key Findings

1. **AI-driven cloud growth**: AI model training and inference workloads are the primary accelerant for IaaS and PaaS growth, with AI-related cloud spending expected to reach $120B+ in 2025.
2. **SaaS remains the largest segment** at $299.1B, driven by AI feature embedding in enterprise applications (Copilot, Einstein GPT, etc.).
3. **Hyperscaler concentration**: AWS (31%), Microsoft Azure (24%), and Google Cloud (12%) command 67% of the IaaS+PaaS market.
4. **Industry cloud** vertical solutions are the fastest-growing SaaS sub-segment, with healthcare cloud at 28% CAGR.

## Regional Distribution (2025 Forecast)

- North America: 51% ($369B)
- Western Europe: 22% ($159B)
- Asia Pacific: 19% ($137B)
- Rest of World: 8% ($58B)

## Strategic Implications

- Enterprises are shifting from "cloud migration" to "AI-native architecture on cloud"
- Multi-cloud strategies are now the norm: 85% of large enterprises use 3+ cloud providers
- Cloud cost optimization (FinOps) has become a top-3 enterprise priority
- Sovereign cloud requirements in EU and APAC are creating new regional market dynamics
